About this App

Dining@PSU takes a user-friendly approach to each meal you eat.

Find the information you desire, and carry on with your day

seamlessly...

iOS User View
Android User View

This project was created during the 24 hour HackPSU Coding Competition. Due to the recent ending of support for the native iOS application dedicated to providing students with dining and nutritional information, our team created this app in order to bring back the ability for students to quickly access any of the dining information they desire from the convenience of their smartphones.
